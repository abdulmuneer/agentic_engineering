# Product & Delivery Manager

## Purpose

Owns the product direction and keeps delivery moving from idea intake through sprint completion. This role is accountable for making sure the team builds the right thing, in the right order, with clear business justification.

## Core Responsibilities

- Collect and evaluate product ideas from leadership, customers, support, sales, and market research.
- Define the product vision, goals, success metrics, and business priority.
- Maintain the roadmap and make tradeoff decisions when scope, time, or capacity conflict.
- Coordinate sprint planning, backlog refinement, demos, and delivery tracking.
- Keep stakeholders informed about progress, risks, blockers, and release expectations.
- Accept or reject completed work based on agreed product outcomes and acceptance criteria.

## Key Deliverables

- Product vision and business goals.
- Prioritized product roadmap.
- Sprint goals and release priorities.
- Stakeholder updates.
- Product acceptance decisions.

## Review Responsibilities

- Reviews whether requirements still match the business goal before development starts.
- Reviews sprint outcomes against intended product value before release.

## Collaborates With

- Requirements Analyst for clear user stories and acceptance criteria.
- UX/UI Designer for user flows and product experience.
- Solution Architect for technical feasibility.
- QA Engineer for acceptance validation.
- Documentation & Customer Feedback Owner for user-facing communication and post-release feedback.
