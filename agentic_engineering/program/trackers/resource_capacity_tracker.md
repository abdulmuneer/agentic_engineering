## Capacity Guidelines

- Reserve capacity for review, testing, support, defects, and deployment work.
- Do not plan every role at full capacity unless production support is handled elsewhere.
- Revisit capacity when urgent work, incidents, or change requests appear.
- Set agentic WIP limits from human review capacity, not from agent availability.
- Reserve explicit capacity for reviewing agent work packets.
