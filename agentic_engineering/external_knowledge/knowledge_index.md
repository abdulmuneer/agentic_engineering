## Promotion Rule

Before adding new knowledge:

- Link the source learning, work packet, research task, or decision.
- Confirm the material is safe to store.
- Record why it matters for future agent or human work.
- Assign an owner and review cadence.

## Review Cadence

Review this index during requirements refinement, architecture design, sprint planning, and retrospectives.
