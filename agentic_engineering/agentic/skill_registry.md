# Skill Registry

Use this registry to track reusable skills, prompts, scripts, and templates promoted from repeated successful work.

## Promotion Criteria

Promote a workflow when:

- It has repeated at least twice or prevented a high-impact failure.
- The inputs and outputs are predictable.
- The evidence requirements are clear.
- The permission class is understood.
- A human reviewer can tell when it succeeded or failed.

## Skill Definition Checklist

- Clear trigger.
- Clear non-goals.
- Required context.
- Allowed tools.
- Stop conditions.
- Output format.
- Verification steps.
- Owner and review cadence.
