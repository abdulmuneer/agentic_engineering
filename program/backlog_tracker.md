## Ready For Sprint Checklist

- The work item links to a requirement, bug, risk, or decision.
- Acceptance criteria are clear.
- Technical approach is understood.
- Dependencies and blockers are known.
- Estimate is agreed by the delivery team.
- Required reviewers are identified.
