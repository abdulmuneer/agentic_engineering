## Requirement Quality Checklist

- The requirement has a clear user or system actor.
- Acceptance criteria are observable and testable.
- Edge cases are documented.
- Security, privacy, accessibility, and operational needs are considered.
- Dependencies are linked.
- The requirement is small enough to plan into sprint work.
